About the Study

This research project, conducted by the Management, Communication & IT department at MCI | The Entrepreneurial School®, explores how students interact with Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) systems. As AI tools become common in academic settings, understanding how to effectively query these systems—and how they present information from specific source texts—is crucial for modern digital literacy. This study specifically evaluates the quality of AI-generated responses based on user prompting behavior. Your interactions will help us understand the synergy between human inquiry and AI data retrieval.

Your participation involves interacting with a chatbot prototype to solve educational tasks. All interactions are anonymized and stored securely for academic research.
