About the Study

This research project, conducted by the Management, Communication & IT department at MCI | The Entrepreneurial School®, explores how students interact with Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) systems. As AI tools become common in academic settings, understanding how to effectively query these systems—and how they present information from specific source texts—is crucial for modern digital literacy. This study specifically evaluates the quality of AI-generated responses based on user prompting behavior. Your interactions will help us understand the synergy between human inquiry and AI data retrieval.

Your participation involves interacting with a chatbot prototype to solve educational tasks. All interactions are anonymized and stored securely for academic research.

Study Instructions

Please follow these steps to complete the study session:

  1. Review the Reference Document: On the right, you will see a document titled "The Future of Remote Work: A Multi-Dimensional Analysis." You do not need to memorize it, but please familiarize yourself with its structure.
  2. Interact with ClarifAI: Once you begin, you will be presented with a chatbot. Your goal is to use the chatbot to answer three specific questions regarding the document's findings.
  3. Evaluate Accuracy: As part of our "Prompting Effectiveness" metric, you will be asked to briefly indicate if the AI successfully retrieved the correct information based on your queries and the provided source text.
  4. Final Survey: After the third interaction, a short exit questionnaire will appear. Completion of this survey is required for your data to be included in the study.

Important Note: To ensure data integrity, please do not refresh your browser during the session. The entire process should take approximately 10–12 minutes.
